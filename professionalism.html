Professionalism in Instruction

Professionalism is the bedrock of trust between an instructor and a pupil.

More Than Just Driving

Being a professional ADI means maintaining high standards in every aspect of your work. It's about your image, your reliability, and how you present the profession to the public.

Professional Appearance

Your attire reflects your professionalism. Wear comfortable yet smart clothing suitable for driving, ensuring you are easily recognizable as an instructor. Avoid overly casual wear to maintain a respectful and authoritative image.

Essential Documents Checklist

Always ensure the following are valid and available during lessons and the Standards Check:

  • Valid Driving Licence (Original)
  • ADI/PDI Badge (Correctly Displayed)
  • Insurance with Instruction Cover
  • MOT & Service Records (for car safety)
  • Pupil Progress Records (Logbooks)
