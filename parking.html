Parking Techniques

Why Parking Skills Matter?
Precision parking is essential for navigating tight urban spaces safely. It demonstrates high-level car control, spatial awareness, and observation, reducing the risk of minor collisions during daily use.

Common Learner Errors

  • Poor Spatial Awareness: Hitting curbs or surrounding cars due to inaccurate reference points.
  • Inadequate Observation: Not checking mirrors or blind spots during the entire reversing manoeuvre.
  • Loss of Control: Reversing too quickly or failing to make necessary steering adjustments in time.
