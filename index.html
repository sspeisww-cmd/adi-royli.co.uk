Your Path to ADI Excellence Starts Here

ADI Ascent is a practical resource hub for PDIs and new instructors—built to make lesson planning, Standards Check prep, and real-world coaching decisions simpler, clearer, and easier to apply.

About Roy Li & Our Mission

Hi, I’m Roy Li—an ADI teaching under Beans Driving School and the AA franchise. I built ADI Ascent because I remember how hard it is at the start: you’re learning the job, learning the wording, and trying to stay safe and professional at the same time.

Many ideas here are inspired by toolbooks and resources that helped me. I’ve reworked them into simpler, more “in-car usable” language—so you can apply it under pressure.

Our Philosophy

Built around Client-Centred Learning— helping pupils think, not just follow instructions.

How to Use This Platform

Use the sidebar like a toolbox. Pick a topic, take a few key questions/phrases, and apply them straight away in the car. If you’re preparing for a Standards Check, work through sections in order and build your “go-to” structure.

Keep it practical: every mistake is data. If you’ve got a real scenario that’s not covered, send it to me—this library keeps evolving to fill real gaps.
