Scenario Handling

Welcome to the Scenario Library. These are examples based on common driving errors, designed to be coached using the F.I.S.H. framework. Driving is unpredictable, so these are just a starting point. Got a unique scenario you’ve faced? We welcome your input to help us keep this library updated and fill in the gaps in our collective knowledge!

F.I.S.H. Coaching Framework

Use these questions to guide your pupil to identify risks and solutions themselves.

Fact
What just happened?
Implication
What is the risk?
Solve
How to fix it?
Help
What support do you need?

Analysis Tool

Roundabout Misjudgment

Click for F.I.S.H. Analysis

Pupil entered roundabout without checking properly, causing a car to brake hard.

FFact: "What did you notice about the speed of that car as you entered?"
IImplication: "What might have happened if they hadn't stopped?"
SSolve: "How could you check for a safer gap next time?"
HHelp: "Want me to talk you through the next one, or try on your own?"

Roundabout Lane Drift

Click for F.I.S.H. Analysis

Pupil drifted out of their lane while turning on a roundabout.

FFact: "Where did you feel the car go in relation to the lane lines?"
IImplication: "What is the risk of drifting into that other lane?"
SSolve: "How can you use your steering to keep the car centered?"
HHelp: "Need a hint on when to start steering, or can you manage it?"

Tailgating

Click for F.I.S.H. Analysis

Pupil was driving too close to the car in front.

FFact: "How many seconds gap do you have right now?"
IImplication: "What will happen if they stop suddenly?"
SSolve: "How can you adjust your speed to increase the gap?"
HHelp: "Shall we practice counting the gap, or do you feel safe here?"

Dangerous Overtaking

Click for F.I.S.H. Analysis

Pupil decided to overtake on a blind bend.

FFact: "What can you see coming around this corner?"
IImplication: "What’s the risk of overtaking right now?"
SSolve: "When will it be safe to overtake?"
HHelp: "Shall we practice looking for hazards together, or do you want a go alone?"

T-Junction Observation

Click for F.I.S.H. Analysis

Pupil looked too early and exited without stopping at a restricted view junction.

FFact: "When did you last check for traffic before moving?"
IImplication: "What could happen if a car was hidden behind that hedge?"
SSolve: "Where is the best point to stop to see clearly?"
HHelp: "Need me to guide your eyes, or do you feel ready to spot them?"

Speed & Control

Click for F.I.S.H. Analysis

Too fast, poor observation and steering control at a junction.

FFact: "How fast did you take that corner?"
IImplication: "How did the speed affect your ability to steer?"
SSolve: "How can you plan your speed better?"
HHelp: "Do you want to practice the approach slowly first?"

Oncoming Traffic Gap

Click for F.I.S.H. Analysis

Misjudged gap with oncoming traffic, tight space when passing.

FFact: "What did you think about the space between us and that truck?"
IImplication: "What would have happened if they didn't slow down?"
SSolve: "How can you decide to stop and wait?"
HHelp: "Want me to help you read the traffic for the next one?"

Poor Observation (Moving Off)

Click for F.I.S.H. Analysis

Pulled away from curb without checking blind spots.

FFact: "What did you check before moving off?"
IImplication: "What’s the risk if a cyclist is beside us?"
SSolve: "How can you make sure it's safe to move?"
HHelp: "Need a prompt, or can you manage the checks?"

Pull Up - Curb Collision

Click for F.I.S.H. Analysis

Hit the curb when pulling up due to poor speed/steering control.

FFact: "Where did the car end up in relation to the curb?"
IImplication: "What damage might we cause to the tires?"
SSolve: "How can you manage your speed to stop smoothly?"
HHelp: "Do you want me to talk you through the steering?"

Late Lane Selection

Left lane selection too late when road widened.

IImplication: "What happens if we can't change lane safely?"
SSolve: "How can you use your planning to move earlier?"

Speeding

Click for F.I.S.H. Analysis

Driving over the speed limit due to poor sign reading.

FFact: "What is the speed limit on this road?"
IImplication: "What’s the risk if we have to brake suddenly?"
SSolve: "How can you keep aware of the limits?"
HHelp: "Do you want me to ask you for the speed limit often?"
